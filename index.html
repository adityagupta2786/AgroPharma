About AgroPharma

AgroPharma is a simple tool for farmers to get fast, expert-backed help for sick crops. Farmers upload a photo, optionally add a short voice note, and receive a suggested diagnosis and recommended treatments.

Our goal is to reduce crop loss and save time. The platform connects quick AI-assisted suggestions with a network of agronomy experts who can provide follow-up advice when needed.

How it helps farmers

Easy Upload

Take a photo or record a short voice note in local language.

Quick Diagnosis

AI provides an instant suggestion; experts review complex cases and reply with treatment steps.

Buy Products

Recommended insecticides and pesticides can be purchased from local retailers through our page.
